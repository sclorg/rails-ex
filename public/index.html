Epidemiology of the Zika Virus

Health app for OpenShift Hackathon contest.

History

First discovered in Uganda in 1947. With a subsequent outbreak in 2007

in Polynesia. However the current outbreak developed in Brazil.

Pathology

Group IV

Flaviviridae

Genus

Hepacivirus

Flavivirus

Pegivirus

Pestivirus

Biology

Spread by vector Mosquito similar to Dengue Fever. Female Aedes aegypti mosquito

which is tree living and widespread.

Diagnosis and Treatment

Carrier may experience no symptoms thus Asymptomatic.

Diagnosed by testing for presence of Zika virus RNA.

Geographic

Africa, French Polynesia, Brazilian states.

Risk

Pregnancy, Possible blood transfusion risk.
